Chicken Noodle Soup

>

Description

This homemade chicken noodle soup is soul-warming and deliciously simple — just chicken and noodles. What makes it so good is the homemade roasted chicken stock.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, carrots, celery, and garlic. Sauté until vegetables are softened.
  2. Pour in chicken broth and add the bay leaf, dried thyme, and dried rosemary. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 15 minutes.
  3. Add shredded chicken and egg noodles to the pot. Cook until noodles are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Lasanga Recipe

Description

Classic Beef Lasagna This classic beef lasagna recipe features layers of tender lasagna noodles, savory ground beef, aromatic onions and garlic, all smothered in a rich tomato sauce. The layers are generously spread with a creamy mixture of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses, creating a comforting and satisfying dish. Baked to perfection, this classic lasagna is a timeless favorite, perfect for gatherings or a cozy family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasanga noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or ground sausage
  • 1 rgg, beaten
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 can tomotoes
  • 2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried basel
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet or saucepan, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic and cook until the onions are softened. Drain excess fat. Stir in the pasta sauce, crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix together the beaten egg, ricotta cheese, and half of the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  5. In a baking dish, layer lasagna as follows:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
    • Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses over the top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy your classic and delicious lasagna! Adjust the ingredients and seasoning to suit your preferences.
